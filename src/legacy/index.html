Our Story

Where Every Detail
Tells Your Story

Marriqa Events was born from a singular belief — that every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. We don't just plan events; we architect emotions, curate aesthetics, and craft memories that endure long after the last guest has departed.

With a deep passion for modern Indian luxury and an eye trained on cinematic beauty, our team brings your most intimate dreams to life. From the intimacy of an engagement to the grandeur of a destination wedding, we approach every project as a unique canvas.

Serving Delhi NCR, Rajasthan, and Uttarakhand — across palaces, resorts, and heritage venues — our signature is precision, warmth, and an uncompromising standard of excellence.
